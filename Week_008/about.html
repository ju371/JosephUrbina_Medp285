About

This archive is inspired by the world of Harry Potter and the magic and symbolism of the horcruxes. The horcruxes are items that Lord Voldemort used to put a piece of his soul in them so that, if he died, he would be able to be brought back as his soul - or pieces of it rather - are still tied to the physical plane and very much alive. And so I remembered that I also have items that are so dear and special to me you could say they have a piece of my soul as well.

In the main page of the archive I have decided to show my own horcruxes with a carousel display and wrote a little bit about them. I wouldn't want to give too much information and reveal their location as their instrumental to my immortality. Beyond my horcruxes you will be able to see a selection of favorite pictures on the left side of the page. And on the right, you can find options to explore different items as well as the original horcruxes from the Harry Potter saga.

This project has become very dear to me as it has allowed me to find objects that were very special to me once and somehow had forgotten due to the crazy routine life we live day by day. It has shown me to also appreciate more the little things for what they are and to realize that even though the "material" stuff is not what makes you happy, it can definitely help you get there by how it makes you feel and the memories each object carries.
