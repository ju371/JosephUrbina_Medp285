  December, 2017 The Wolf Canvas
A.K.A. The Crimson Fire Wolf

This canvas is by far one of the most precious pieces of art I own. It was given to me by a dear friend on Christmas day and have cherished it ever since. The wolf is the representation of my spirit animal and favorite animal, period.

  July, 2013 Plushie Shark
A.K.A. Tiburonzin Uh Haha

This plushie was won on my birthday at the amusement park near where I used to live in Venezuela. It was the month before I came to this country, so the last month I spent in my country enjoying a good time with my friends.

  August, 2019 The "Have Fun" Coconut
A.K.A. Happy Coconut

This coconut was purchased in Miami the night before the end of our roadtrip down in South Beach, Miami. It was offered to us in the restaurant by the waiters and my friend and I just took them. It serves as an excellent cup holder.

  March, 2018 The Guitar
A.K.A. EPIPHONE

I bought this guitar at a time where I needed music the most and wanted to get back to playing instruments. I don't really know how to play professionaly, but I do like to play to the beat of some of my favorite songs. I have been feeling more and more inspired to play more lately and I am excited to try newer songs.

  December 2010/ January 2018 The First Reads
A.K.A. The first books I read in Spanish and English respectively

The Blue Book "The Ingenious Games" was gifted to me on Christmas and was one of the first books that kickstarted my passion for books. Filled with mystery and murder games it kept me trapped until the very end. The second book, "Fahrenheit 451" was the one that I was required in my class MEDP 180 and loved every bit of it. Even more when the movie came out and got to read it again.

  June 2019 The "Pins" Shirt
A.K.A. The most expensive shirt I own

I bought this shirt in a Festival Expo of Pins and Patches in Downtown, New York. Each pin varies in prices and rarity according to size and place of origin. The shirt exhibits mostly characters from Spiderman, Avengers, Naruto, Star Wars, Undertale, Egypt and Adobe Illustrator.
